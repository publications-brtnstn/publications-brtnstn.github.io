Experimental informatics generates the hypothesis.

Most informatics starts with data. We start with a question. Through simulation, computational analysis, and close collaboration with the bench, _exp_i_ labs produces testable hypotheses — then hands them off to be tested.

This is hypothesis-driven informatics. Not descriptive. Not reactive. Experimental.

We collaborate with molecular biology and clinical laboratories — bringing the hypothesis to the bench, and the data back to the model.
